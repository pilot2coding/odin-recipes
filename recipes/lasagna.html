Classic Italian Meat Lasagna

Acclaimed as one of the most delicious italian cuisine dishes.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Fresh parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Parmesan
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta
  • Eggs
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before eating.
