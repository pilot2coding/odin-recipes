Eggplant Baba Ganoush

A silky smooth eggplant based hummus.

Ingredients:

  • Eggplant
  • Tahini
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic

Steps:

  1. Roast the eggplant and let it cool.
  2. Scoop out the the flesh from the eggplant.
  3. Mix the eggplant with the other ingredients.
  4. Mix in the olive oil.
  5. Let it chill before serving and enjoy!
